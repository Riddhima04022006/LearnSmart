About LearnSmart

Designed to help students focus on what actually matters.

What LearnSmart Does

LearnSmart helps students identify important and frequently repeated topics from previous years’ exam papers. It reduces cognitive overload by allowing students to focus on high-priority areas.

How to Use LearnSmart

  1. Upload scanned PDFs or images of previous exam papers.
  2. Enter the subject syllabus in the provided text area.
  3. The system extracts and analyzes repeated questions.
  4. A structured list of important topics is generated.

Disclaimer

LearnSmart is an assistive study tool. OCR and AI-based analysis may contain inaccuracies. Students should always verify results with official syllabi and textbooks.

This tool does not guarantee exam outcomes and should be used only as a supplementary aid.
